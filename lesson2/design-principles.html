White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com/za/

Apple's use of white space, or negative space, gives each element enough space, allowing the page to not feel cramped or over crowded. This improves the readability of the page and makes the page appear to be more professional.

Contrast

Microsoft

microsoft.com/en-za

The use of a predominantly white background and black text and buttons creates a stark contrast on this page. This use of contrast draws the user's attention to the buttons making them appear to be more important than the other elements on the page.

Alignment

NVIDIA

nvidia.com/en-us/

This page has a very sleek and clean look to it through the use of alignment. All of the text is in line on the left of the page, making it easy to read and making the page easy to follow and navigate. The on elements that are not aligned are the headings, this makes them stand out and causes the user to read them first.
